## Lesson for next shift — cap parallel subagents at 2-3

The 8-agent burst worked but was over the local capacity ceiling. Each worktree runs its own `just test`, and `.bazelrc` already pins `--local_test_jobs=4` — so 8 agents thrash the FDB container budget rather than scale. Effective concurrency was probably 3-4 with the rest queued.

**Recommended cap**: 2-3 parallel agents when each runs `just test`. Or: have agents skip local verification entirely and let the integrator batch-test the combined patch — cheaper than N×full-suite runs that share a 4-container ceiling. Either way, don't spawn 8 in a single message again.
