### Investigation findings

Three approaches attempted for the full migration. All hit the same root cause: `PlanningCostModelLess` is order-sensitive for cost-equal plans (ties go to first member in Reference). Changing when/how physical wrappers are inserted changes which plan wins for queries with cost-equivalent alternatives.

**Approach 1: Adapter (BatchA as ImplementationRules in PLANNING)**
- FinalMembers gets multiple physical alternatives from adapted rules
- `bestPhysicalFrom(finals)` picks first-in-order, not cost-best
- Cost-based selection breaks derived-table queries (cost model's child estimation unreliable for fresh References from MemoizeExpression)

**Approach 2: Two-phase explore (BatchA in second Explore pass)**
- Physical wrappers in Members, OPTIMIZE stamps them correctly
- Derived-table + GROUP BY + indexed column regression: member insertion ordering differs from single-phase, changing GetBest tie-breaking for streaming-agg alternatives

**Approach 3: FinalMembers preference in extraction**
- Skip physical expressions in FinalizeExpressionsRule (correct)
- Prefer FinalMembers physical plans over EXPLORE bestMember
- FinalizeExpressionsRule still wraps EXPLORE physical wrappers with stale disentangled children → wrong plans selected

### Key discovery

The singular blocker for the migration is NOT the extraction logic or the adapter — it's the **cost model's determinism**. `PlanningCostModelLess` has 17 comparison criteria, but criteria 1-15 often tie for alternative physical plans over the same logical expression. Criterion 16 (EstimateCost scalar) sometimes differs due to child Reference structure changes. Criterion 17 (hash tiebreak) is deterministic but only fires when EstimateCost also ties.

The fix path: ensure `EstimateCost` produces consistent results regardless of whether child References are shared (from Memo) or fresh (from MemoizeExpression). This requires `firstMemberCostMemoised` to handle fresh References consistently — either by indexing FinalMembers in the Memo or by using a fallback cost for References with no Members.
